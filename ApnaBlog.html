RBI Digital Rupee- How to Avail and Use e-Rupee?
The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) launched a digital rupee pilot in four cities. The primary goal of introducing digital rupees is to eventually phase out paper money, lowering the cost of managing and transporting paper money. Digital Rupee or e-Rupee has no connection to cryptocurrency and is not based on Blockchain technology. Central bank Digital currency (CBDC) is a digital form of cash or paper money. That also means that the digital Rupee and paper money have the same value. Read More
